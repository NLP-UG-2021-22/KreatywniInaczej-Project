About SearchOnce

Our application was developed as part of an introduction to a web development course. We were keen not to create something fully reconstructive, but something that we ourselves could actually use. This is how we came up with the idea of creating multiple English dictionaries in one.

Above all, such a solution saves time and is visually more convenient. You no longer have to type one entry repeatedly in several online dictionaries or open several new pages — you can search just once.
